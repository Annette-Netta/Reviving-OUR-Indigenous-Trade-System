WELCOME to OUR Website for Indigenous People

OUR Ways are Strong and Sure.

Our Ways include sharing what we have across the lands.

Remember a time when our Ancestors had an abundance of goods and they traded with others?

Our people traded so we could share our resources and procure the resources we did not have. Many miles on land and water were travelled to secure the needed supplies.

Wherever we live on these lands, there is an abundance that Mother Earth provides for us.

Some of us have:
  • fish
  • moose
  • caribou
  • fish oils
  • wild chicken
  • berries
  • fruits
  • vegetables
  • roots

Some of us NEED:
  1. fresh foods from the land
  2. clean healthy meats
  3. wild berries for our vitamins
  4. medicines of the land

What can we do?

Connect and listen to your spirit and know that this is the time that we decide to remember and revive OUR Trade Systems for ourself, our families, our communities, and our Nations.

This is a place for you to come and find the fresh, wholesome supplies from the land and water that nurture your body, spirit, mind, and soul.
